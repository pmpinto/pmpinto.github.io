Hello!

My name is Pedro Pinto and I’m a mix of a designer + developer from Portugal, currently working at Doist.

Developing websites is both fun and challenging. Not only through the design process, but also the implementation. I get to regularly try new approaches and do things I haven't done before.

Interfaces that are appealing and easy to use, hooked with powerful and modern technologies are always a pleasure to work on. That’s what I’ve been focusing on for the past decade.

Other than geek stuff, I also spend my time on photography, listening to music, riding 2 and 4 wheeled vehicles, cooking, hiking, doing the mandatory exercise and playing table tennis.
